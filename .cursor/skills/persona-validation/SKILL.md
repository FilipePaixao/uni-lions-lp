# Persona Validation

## Workflow

For each persona in `knowledge/audience/personas.md`:

1. Enter with the persona's device, knowledge level, and urgency.
2. Answer within the rendered page:
   - What is this?
   - Is it for me?
   - Why now?
   - What do I leave with?
   - Who is behind it?
   - How much time and money?
   - What happens after I apply?
3. Record where the answer was found and how long it took.
4. Attempt the primary conversion path.
5. Identify trust gaps, jargon, missing proof, or mobile friction.
6. Score clarity, relevance, trust, effort, and next-step confidence from 1–5.

## Guardrails

Do not simulate demographic stereotypes. Use persona context only to test needs, constraints, and comprehension.
